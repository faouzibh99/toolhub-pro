Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ToolHub Pro ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit toolhubpro.com (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The tools on this Site — including the Word Counter, character counter, and text-formatting utilities — run entirely in your web browser. Any text you type or paste into these tools is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers, stored, or analyzed by us. We have no access to the content of your text.

2. Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, our Site may automatically collect certain information when you visit, including:

  • Your IP address (used for general location detection and security purposes)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring website
  • Date and time of your visit

This information is collected through cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies described below.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to help the Site function and to collect analytics and advertising data. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site functionality, such as remembering your dark mode preference.
  • Analytics cookies: Used by Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site (pages visited, time on site, traffic sources).
  • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense and other third-party advertising partners to display relevant ads and measure ad performance.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features on the Site.

4. Google AdSense

This Site uses Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this Site or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this Site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You may also opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.

5. Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to monitor and analyze traffic to our Site. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit the Site, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming to this Site. We use the information we get from Google Analytics only to improve this Site. Google Analytics collects only the IP address assigned to you on the date you visit this Site, rather than your name or other identifying information. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices by visiting Google's Privacy Policy.

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not owned or controlled by ToolHub Pro. We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding the personal information collected about you, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.
